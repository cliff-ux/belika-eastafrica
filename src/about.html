Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Belika East Africa Solutions began as a small IT equipment supplier in Nairobi with a vision to transform technology access across East Africa. What started as a modest operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted technology solution providers.

Over the years, we've established partnerships with global technology leaders and built a reputation for reliability, exceptional customer service, and deep technical expertise. Our journey reflects the rapid technological advancement of East Africa itself.

Our Mission

To empower businesses, organizations, and institutions across East Africa with premium IT infrastructure solutions that drive digital transformation, innovation, and sustainable growth in an increasingly connected world.

Our Vision

To be the leading IT solutions provider in East Africa, recognized for exceptional quality, unparalleled technical expertise, and our commitment to fueling Africa's digital revolution through accessible, reliable technology.
